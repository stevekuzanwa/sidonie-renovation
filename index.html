OUR COMPANY

At SIDONIE Renovation, we have years of experience both as project managers and building surveyors. Working with local tradesman to deliver the highest quality build and finish to every project.
We use local joiners to create beautifully crafted fitted kitchens and bathrooms and as the company director’s we are always on hand to offer advice and support from the start of your build project to the finish.
The key to the success of one of the components and quality assurance of SODONIE Renovation is a professional highly qualified staff that clearly knows the range of their responsibilities and excellently manages them.
They are ready to answer all customer questions, fundamentally renovate to the high quality efficiently and professionally by providing services for the construction of houses, cottages, properties, offices and other facilities, as well as buildings.
