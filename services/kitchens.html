Kitchens

ABOUT THE SERVICE

They say the kitchen is the ‘heart of the home’. We believe a kitchen not only needs to be functional, but light, comfortable, warm and inviting.
We install kitchens of the highest quality and provide exceptional workmanship and finish. When it comes to kitchen renovations and extensions, we pride ourselves on meeting our clients expectations and hopefully exceeding them. We’ll help you all the way from planning and design right through to completion in order to create an amazing kitchen for your home.

We work with several suppliers to ensure we obtain the best match for both our clients’ budget and preferences. We can also make you a fully bespoke kitchen if you prefer with hand made units. Needless to say we are also able to help in your selection of fixtures & fittings, tiles and solid or composite worksurfaces. We can also help you select appliances, sinks and taps from a wide selection of brands including Miele, Bosch, Neff, Siemens, Franke, Gaggenau, Villeroy & Boch, Sub Zero, Wolf.
