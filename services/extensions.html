ABOUT THE SERVICE

House extensions are a great way to maximise your space and increase the value of your home. An extension requires a team of qualified tradespeople, with every type of skill from the architect to the setting of the foundation, through to painting and decorating at the final stage of the project.

At SIDONIE Renovation we pride ourselves on our ability to take the stress out of extending your home. That’s why if you decide to convert your loft or extend your property we can offer a complete design and build service. We will arrange a meeting with our architect to discuss the best design for your property. We will then coordinate any necessary applications for Planning Permission, Party Wall Agreements and Building Control. Once all the designs and permissions are complete we will commence the work from structural alterations to final decoration.
