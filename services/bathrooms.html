Bathrooms

ABOUT THE SERVICE

It’s true what they say; you spend more waking hours of your life in the bathroom than any other. With today’s ever increasing workloads and stress levels, it more important than ever to have an oasis you can retreat to and regain your sense of calm and direction.

One of our most important considerations within our bathroom works relates to the care and attention to detail in the installation stage to ensure that all wet areas are suitably tanked to reduce any water-related issues in the future. We work with several suppliers to ensure we obtain the best match for both our clients’ budgets and tastes. Needless to say we are also able to help in your selection of fixtures & fittings and tiles. We can also help you select baths, sinks and toilets from a wide selection of brands. A few of our faviourites include Duravit, Crosswater, Aqualisa, Matki, Roca, Hansgrohe, Grohe and Geberit concealed cisterns.
